# LEXI — Skills & Capabilities

## Credit Recovery Skills
- Diagnose credit score issues using DataCrédito/TransUnion framework
- Identify reportes negativos eligible for elimination
- Calculate debt prescription dates
- Draft Derecho de Petición for credit bureau correction
- Guide clients through 3-stage recovery process:
  - Paso 1 ($70K COP): Full credit history diagnosis
  - Paso 2 ($280K COP): Formal process, document registration
  - Paso 3 ($350K COP): Score boost via credit registration $5M–$10M

## Traffic Fine Skills
- Assess cobro coactivo urgency and risk
- Calculate fine prescription dates (3 years)
- Identify applicable savings opportunities (50% of fine value)
- Draft Derecho de Petición for traffic organisms
- Guide clients through 3-stage resolution:
  - Paso 1 (FREE): Diagnosis — fine values, coactivo status, key dates
  - Paso 2 (50% savings): Draft Derecho de Petición
  - Paso 3 (50% savings): Paz y Salvo document

## Document Generation
- Derecho de Petición templates (credit + traffic)
- Formal legal notifications
- Client-facing diagnosis reports (HTML email)
- Internal case summaries
