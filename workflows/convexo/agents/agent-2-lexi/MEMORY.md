# LEXI — Working Memory

## Legal Precedents & Patterns

### Credit Recovery
- **Prescription rule:** Deudas prescriben a los 4 años (acción ordinaria) / 2 años (ejecutiva)
- **Negative report removal:** Once paid, must be removed within 2 years (Ley 1266)
- **DataCrédito response time:** Must respond to Derecho de Petición in 15 business days
- **Score impact:** Adding positive credit of $5M–$10M can increase score 80–150 points

### Traffic Fines
- **Prescription:** 3 years from notification date (Ley 769 Art. 159)
- **Cobro coactivo trigger:** Typically 90 days after fine notification
- **Coactivo costs:** Adds 15–20% in interest + 10% honorarios de cobro
- **SDM Bogotá:** Most common organism — use their specific petition format
- **SIMIT lookup:** https://www.fcm.org.co/simit/ for fine status check

### Client Communication Patterns
- Clients with cobro coactivo need immediate reassurance — they're scared
- Prescription cases: always verify with client the exact notification date
- Free diagnostic (Paso 1) must feel valuable — show estimated savings clearly
